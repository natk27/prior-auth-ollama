PRIOR AUTHORIZATION POLICY
======================================================================

Procedure: CT Scan Abdomen
CPT Code: 74177

======================================================================

**Policy Document**

**PROCEDURE OVERVIEW**

* Procedure Name: CT Scan Abdomen
* CPT Code: 74177

The CT Scan Abdomen procedure is a diagnostic imaging service used to visualize the internal structures of the abdomen, including organs such as the liver, kidneys, and intestines. This procedure helps healthcare providers diagnose and monitor various abdominal conditions, including injuries, diseases, and cancers.

**COVERAGE CRITERIA**

To be covered by our insurance plan, all of the following criteria must be met:

1. **Conservative Treatment Requirements**: The patient must have attempted conservative treatment for at least 12 weeks before receiving a CT Scan Abdomen procedure.
2. **Symptom Duration Requirements**: The patient's symptoms must have persisted or worsened for a minimum of 6 weeks prior to the procedure.
3. **Clinical Documentation Required**: The patient's medical records must include:
	* A detailed history and physical examination
	* Diagnostic test results, including previous imaging studies (e.g., X-rays, MRIs)
	* Treatment logs, including conservative treatment plans and any failed therapies
4. **Imaging Requirements**: Previous imaging studies, including X-rays or MRIs of the abdomen, must be completed within the past 6 months.
5. **Severity Criteria**:
	* The patient's pain level must be at least moderate (on a scale of 1-10) to meet coverage criteria
	* Functional assessments must demonstrate significant impairment in daily activities

**MEDICAL NECESSITY CRITERIA**

To ensure medical necessity, the following criteria must be met:

1. **Patient Selection Criteria**: The patient must have:
	* A confirmed abdominal diagnosis or suspected condition that requires imaging guidance
	* A history of failed conservative treatment for their symptoms
2. **Failed Prior Treatments**: The patient's symptoms must have worsened despite previous treatments, including conservative therapies and medications.
3. **Contraindications to Conservative Care**: The patient must have:
	* Severe abdominal pain that cannot be managed with conservative treatments
	* Significant impairment in daily activities due to abdominal symptoms

**EXCLUSION CRITERIA**

The following exclusions apply:

1. **Active Infections**: Patients with active infections, such as appendicitis or diverticulitis, are not eligible for coverage.
2. **Pregnancy (if applicable)**: Pregnant women are not eligible for coverage unless specifically approved by our insurance plan.
3. **Severe Comorbidities**: Patients with severe comorbidities, such as liver disease or kidney failure, may be excluded from coverage if their symptoms cannot be managed effectively with conservative treatments.
4. **Lack of Conservative Treatment**: Patients who have not attempted conservative treatment for at least 12 weeks prior to the procedure are not eligible for coverage.
5. **Cosmetic Indications**: The patient's symptoms must be related to a medical condition, not cosmetic concerns.

**DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS**

To support our review process, the following documentation is required:

1. **History and Physical Examination**: A comprehensive history and physical examination report within 6 months prior to the procedure.
2. **Diagnostic Test Results**: Previous imaging studies, including X-rays or MRIs of the abdomen, completed within the past 6 months.
3. **Treatment Logs**: Detailed treatment logs, including conservative therapy plans and any failed therapies.
4. **Specialist Consultations**: Records of specialist consultations, including radiology reports and diagnosis statements.

**AUTHORIZATION DETAILS**

* Validity: This policy is valid for 90 days from the date of approval.
* Peer-to-Peer Review: If coverage is denied, a peer-to-peer review will be conducted to determine medical necessity.
